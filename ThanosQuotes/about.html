Thanos Quotes

Thanos is a fictional character that appears in Marvel Comics publications. The character's name is a derivation of Thanatos, the personification of death and mortality in Greek mythology. IMO he is the greatest supervillain of all time, as a fan of his, I have decided to put up this litle app using basic Vanilla Javascript skills that I have learnt over the past few days. Enjoy some of his quotes from the MCU.
